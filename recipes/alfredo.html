Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Creamy Alfredo sauce tossed with tender chicken and fettuccine pasta.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breasts
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Heavy cream
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, minced
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook chicken in olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and set aside.
  2. Cook fettuccine pasta al dente.
  3. For the sauce, saut√© garlic in a pan, add heavy cream, and bring to a simmer. Stir in Parmesan until melted.
  4. Combine pasta, chicken, and Alfredo sauce. Serve warm.
