Steak Sandwich

Description

A freshly toasted ribeye steak sandwich with cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Steak
  • Cheese
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Take 1tbsp of butter and throw it on the pan. Heat pan until butter is melted
  2. Toast bread on the pan
  3. Spread mayo on bread while in the pan
  4. Add cheese and let it melt. Remove bread from pan
  5. Cut steak into small chunks
  6. Fit steak neatly onto sandwich
  7. Add lettuce and tomato. Enjoy!
