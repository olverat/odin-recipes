Baked Mac and Cheese

Description

Rich, creamy macaroni, baked to golden perfection with a crispy breadcrumb topping.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni.
  2. In a separate pot, make a roux with butter and flour.
  3. Add milk to roux, then cheese. Stir until smooth.
  4. Mix sauce with pasta, top with breadcrumbs.
  5. Bake until bubbly and golden.
